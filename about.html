About Stride

At Stride, we make shoes that move with you — thoughtfully designed, responsibly sourced, and built for everyday life. We blend performance, comfort, and style so your footwear fits every part of your day.

Our Story

Born from a love of movement and the pursuit of comfort, Stride started as a small workshop and grew into a brand that values design and durability. We partner with ethical manufacturers and choose materials that last — because a great shoe should reduce waste, not create it.

What We Make

From breathable everyday runners to classic leather silhouettes and rugged trail shoes, our collections are crafted to cover real life. Each design balances fit, grip, and finish so you don't have to compromise.
